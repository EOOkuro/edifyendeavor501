About Edify

Edify is a 501(c)3 nonprofit that got its start in 2019. Our focus is to provide safe spaces and opportunities for youth to develop holistically. With this, we focus on ways to support individuals to grow personally, professionally, and intellectually.

At Edify, we believe everyone deserves a quality education and opportunities for personal and professional advancement. We are aware that the cycles of poverty and oppression often deny individuals of color these rights. It is our goal to dismantle these cycles and uplift those who have been affected.
